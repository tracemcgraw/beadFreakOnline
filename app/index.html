Our Philosophy

Art Saves Lives

We at Bead Freak Online believe art is more than just something you just wear, listen to, or see.

It a valuable tool we should use everyday to challenge ourselves to be better and to show the world what we can do.

When you wear our jewelry from Bead Freak Online you are getting just that. We challenge the norm by making all of our jewelry hand made with all recycled material we take for granted. Why buy from manufracture poluting fashion when you can say no to fast fashion and hello to a new type of fashion. A type of fashion built on love, and innovation.

About us

What is life without art?

We are a small jewelry company located in Indianapolis, IN. Our commitment to art is as well as fashion is what sets us apart from the rest. Our work is consitantly changing and developing, so what you see today maybe gone the next. When you see our jewelry on you just know that our hands created that from things we discard. What we think is trash could very well be a gift to cheer up a friend, or to remined someone just how much they mean to you. We also offer contract pieces specifically designed for you. We do this not for money, but to make someone's day a little better and easier. We truly believe that art saves lives!

Renee Hay, Founder and CEO of Bead Freak Online
